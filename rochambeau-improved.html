About This Visualization

In the summer and fall of 1781, approximately 17,000 Franco-American troops converged on Yorktown in one of history’s most complex joint operations. This enhanced visualization traces all six parallel elements of the march.

New features: Responsive on phones/tablets • Animated play-through • Deep links you can share • Full keyboard navigation.

How to Use (v2)

Drag or swipe the timeline • Click Play to watch the full campaign animate • Click any node for details and map zoom • Use ?camp=ID or ?date=YYYY-MM-DD in the URL for direct links • Tab + Enter works on nodes • Share button copies a link to your current view.
