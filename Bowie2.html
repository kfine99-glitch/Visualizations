Strange Chronicles of the Race Track

Bowie Race Track accumulated a history that goes well beyond its racing results.

1935: The Daily Double Bowie premiers the Daily Double in Maryland, changing betting history forever.
1955: The Infield Cruiser Officials discover a full-sized cabin cruiser floating in the infield lake. No one ever explains how it got there.
1966: The Great Escape During the Blizzard of '66, 100 horses are turned loose. Some are later found wandering the Belair Shopping Center.
1972: Noah's Ark International A race featuring a zebu, a llama, and a camel. A buffalo named "Home on the Range" refuses to finish.
