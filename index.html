Solar System Explorer

Domain: Space / astronomy Mode: 3D time-based simulation

Interactive heliocentric simulator that lets you orbit the Sun alongside the planets, moons, and deep-space probes to feel the real scale, timing, and geometry of the solar system.

Includes all eight planets with realistic orbital periods and rotation, 14 key moons, and 12 historic probes from Pioneer and Voyager through New Horizons, Juno, Dawn, Rosetta, and Parker Solar Probe. Time controls let you play, pause, reverse, and change simulation speed, use date search to jump to any calendar date, and focus the camera on any object while toggling orbits, moons, and probe trajectories.

US Military Aircraft Evolution

Span: 1917 – present Focus: Aircraft lineages & eras

Timeline map of American military aviation, from WWI biplanes through strategic bombers, carrier air wings, transports, rotary-wing aircraft, and the full UAV/drone lineage.

Nodes represent significant aircraft families across eight lanes (USAF/Army, naval aviation, attack/CAS, bombers, transport, flying boats, rotary wing, and UAVs), with gold lines for Republic Thunderbolt lineage and teal for the Maryland-built Martin flying boat/bomber chain. Filters for service, nation, conflict era, and UAVs, plus an “Active at year” slider and Surprise button, help you see which aircraft were flying in any year and trace design evolution and influence.

US Ground Vehicle Evolution

Span: 1915 – present Focus: Armor & support

Evolution of US ground combat power, from WWI trucks and motorcycles through the Sherman and Patton families, M1 Abrams, Bradley, MRAPs, HIMARS, and the new M10 Booker light tank.

Covers 89 vehicles across ten lanes including wheeled recon, light/medium/heavy tanks and TDs, SP artillery and MLRS, trucks and logistics, recovery/engineering, APCs/IFVs/LVTs, and missile TELs. Category, nation, and conflict-era filters isolate families like NATO Cold War armor, Lend-Lease exports, and Sparrows Point–armored vehicles, while the year slider and lineage highlighting show what was fielded in WWII, Vietnam, Cold War, Gulf War, and OIF/OEF (Operation Iraqi Freedom and Operation Enduring Freedom).

US Naval Vessel Evolution

Span: 1895 – 2030 Focus: Fleet composition

A 135‑year view of American sea power, from pre‑dreadnought battleships through nuclear supercarriers, ballistic‑missile submarines, modern destroyers, amphibs, auxiliaries, and Coast Guard cutters.

Twelve lanes cover battleships, carriers, cruisers, destroyers, escorts/frigates, submarines, amphibious ships, auxiliaries, Coast Guard, small combatants, naval/Marine helicopters, and carrier aviation classes. Cyan lines trace nuclear propulsion from USS Nautilus onward, gold lines show conversions (like battlecruiser hulls to carriers), and filters plus “Active at year” let you see the exact fleet on dates such as Pearl Harbor, D‑Day, or the Cuban Missile Crisis, with ship‑level fates and NavSource links in the variants tab.

US Weapons Systems Evolution

Span: 1890 – present Focus: Individual & crew‑served

Dense lineage map of 115 US weapons systems across all four services and SOCOM, from Krag bayonets and early revolvers through the M4A1, Barrett .50, Javelin, TOW, modern mortars, artillery, grenades, mines, and scatterable systems.

Thirteen lanes cover bladed weapons and flamethrowers, handguns, rifles and carbines, SMGs, MGs, mortars, AT rockets/recoilless, MANPADS, pack and field artillery, grenades/demolitions, sniper/precision rifles, rotary/rapid‑fire guns, and mines/area‑denial. Category, branch, and era filters, plus Lend‑Lease/export and “Active at year,” highlight service‑unique weapons, export stories, and evolutionary chains, with a legend explaining lineage, shared‑platform, and influence connections across a century of US small arms and support weapons.
